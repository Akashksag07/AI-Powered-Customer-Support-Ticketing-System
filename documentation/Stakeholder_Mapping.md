# Stakeholder Mapping

## Project Name
AI-Powered Customer Support Ticketing System

| Stakeholder | Role | Responsibility |
|-------------|------|----------------|
| Customers | End User | Raise support tickets and track their status. |
| Support Agents | User | Resolve customer issues and update ticket status. |
| Support Manager | Supervisor | Monitor support performance and manage agents. |
| Salesforce Administrator | System Admin | Configure objects, flows, users, and security. |
| Organization | Business | Improve customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. |

## Stakeholder Benefits
- Faster issue resolution.
- Automated email notifications.
- Better tracking of customer support cases.
- Improved reporting and analytics.
